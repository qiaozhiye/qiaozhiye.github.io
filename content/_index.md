Welcome to my website!

I am an Assistant Professor of Finance at the Dishui Lake Advanced Finance Institute, Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE-DAFI). My research focuses on institutional investors, corporate governance, sustainable finance, and financial regulation.

I earned a PhD in Finance from the National University of Singapore (NUS). Before that, I received a Bachelor of Commerce degree (1st-Class Honours) from the University of Queensland (UQ) and a Bachelor of Business Administration degree from the Guangdong University of Foreign Studies (GDUFS). I was awarded the UQ University Medal in 2019 and was a visiting scholar at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) in 2023. Feel free to contact me via email: yeqiaozhi@sufe.edu.cn or qiaozhi.ye@u.nus.edu.
